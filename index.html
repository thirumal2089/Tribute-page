SWAMI VIVEKANANDA

Great philosopher, Hindu monk, and spiritual leader who believed in the mantra of simple living and high thinking

 

TIMELINES

  • JAN 12, 1863 | Birth
    -born on January 12, Monday, in Calcutta, India as Narendra Nath Dutta.
  • JAN 1, 1869 | Early School
    -At the age of 6, Swami Vivekananda goes to pathshala, a school where kids learn Sanskrit.
  • JAN 1, 1871 | 2nd Grade
    -entered the Metropolitan Institute.
  • JAN 1, 1879 | College
    -went to the Presidency College (courtesy of oldkolkata.in)
  • NOV 1, 1881 | 8th Grade
    -transfered to the General Assembly Institution (courtesy of puronokolkata.com)
  • JUL 1, 1881 | Meeting Sri Ramakrishna
    -While in the General Assembly Insitution, Swami Vivekananda met Sri Ramakrishna.
  • AUG 16, 1886 | Sri Ramakrishna Dies
    -Sri Ramakrishna dies due to throat failure
  • JAN 10, 1897 | The Ramakrishna Mission is Founded
    -created the Ramakrishna Mission, an organization that focuses on different social services.
  • SEP 10, 1899 | Belur Math is founded
    -created Belur Math, an organization that focused on monastic life of monks.
  • SEP 11, 1893 | Arrival in Chicago
    -arrived in Chicago to represent Hinduism and deliver his message to the other cultures.
  • JUL 4, 1902 | Death
    -At the age of just 39, Swami Vivekananda died.
